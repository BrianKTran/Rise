The Counsel Of God

Jeremiah 29:11

For I know the Thoughts I have for you, they are not of evil but to give you properous plans and a future of Hope. Gods Word in your life will Impart Faith as you take the time to meditate on i it daily day and night. His grace is sufficeint for you to dream again and by the blood of Jesus conquer as you hunger and thirst after righteousness. We all are gifted in the Body of Christ but it takes time,seasons,a journey of faith to grow in the gifts for the edification of the Body. The prophets corner consist of fresh bread from God given in our lives for certian seasons in our lives to strengthen , lead, guide , and much more. Many teachings on this page will enlighten our eyes through Gods for the greater things he has instored for us and for the knowing of the times and seasons we are in. In all our getting get an understanding for the expansion of the kingdom.

Break the Chain

THE HARD TRUTH

The Body of Christ need to embrace prophecy and encourage the gifts in the Body. Some churches because of experiences are skeptical but we need to encourage the gifts to operate and is okay for churches to birth other ministries as long as it is done in decentcy and in order. The expansion of the kingdom consist at times of sending others out in the mission field or to build churches and ministries in other areas as the Lord calls and appoints. If we are called into a ministry we need a covering and need to work with the Body. God does not call us apart to work by ourselves , accountability is good. The disciples were accountable to one another to Jesus and the apostles as well.

Many times a prophet is without Honor in his own company as scriptures teaches us.

Prophets are still in the land today in the 21 century.

Greater Glory -We are Carriers of Gods Glory

In John 17 Jesus prayed for the Body to the father that the same glory be in us and with us . That same glory is upon us and in us , the body of Christ. Not a title, not a few christians, not just the apostle , prophet or pastor this Glory is upon and in all beleivers and followers of Jesus Christ. Jesus is soon to come back and he is constantly leading us in all perfection in him for he is coming back for a Glorious Holy church. Stay ready, Watch and pray, stay sober be Vigilant!!! Walk in the Unity and love in the Body , love your enemies pray for those that persecute you and know that our battle is not agianst flesh and blood.


FAITH WORKS THROUGH LOVE

All things are possible for those that believes in the day God leads us to dream again.

Run in the God kind of Faith!

As we walk this journey we can have the Assurance that better and greater things are coming and are ours.

Fix your heart to believe in Faith all that Gods word says concerning you. Hold on to his promises keep them before your eyes and run with his word hid in your heart and close your ears to the negativity of the multitude. Believe the report of the Lord and at times you may have to even close your ears to those closest to you if Faith is not flowing through their lips. Fight the good fight of Faith, love those that can't believe with you but guard your faith. Guard your faith by meditating day and night on the word of God , declare his word daily and when the pressure or fight is on speak the Word only. Be steadfast unmoveable, keep your praise, don't shut your mouth, open it wide to parise the Lord Jesus Christ the Bishop of our souls.

Psalms 1

Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, not standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.

But his delight is in the law of the Lord; and in his law doth he meditate day and night.

And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his fruit in his season, his leaf also shall not wither, and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.
